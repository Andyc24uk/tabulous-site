Privacy Policy

Tabulous is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, transmit, store, or sell any personal data.

Data Collection

Tabulous does not collect any personal information, browsing history, or identifiable user data.

All workspace data (such as workspace names, colors, and tab URLs) is stored locally on your device using Chrome’s built-in storage APIs. This data never leaves your browser.

Data Usage

The data stored by Tabulous is used solely to provide its core functionality, including organizing workspaces, restoring windows, and recovering sessions after unexpected browser shutdowns.

Tabulous does not analyze, share, or transmit this data in any way.

Permissions

Tabulous requires certain Chrome permissions to function correctly:

  • Tabs and Windows – to organize, move, and restore browser tabs and windows.
  • Storage – to save workspace information locally on your device.
  • Alarms – to periodically save recovery snapshots so your workspaces can be restored in the event of a browser crash or unexpected shutdown.

These permissions are used only for core functionality and never for tracking, analytics, or advertising.

Third Parties

Tabulous does not use third-party analytics, tracking services, advertising networks, or external APIs.

Data Retention

All data remains on your local device and can be removed at any time by uninstalling the extension.
