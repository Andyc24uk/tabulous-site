Privacy Policy

Tabulous is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, transmit, store on external servers, or sell personal data.

Data collection

Tabulous does not collect personal information, browsing history, or identifiable user data for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

Local storage only

Workspace data (such as workspace names, colors, and tab URLs) is stored locally on your device using Chromeâ€™s built-in storage APIs (chrome.storage). This information remains on your browser profile and is not sent externally.

Permissions

Tabulous requests permissions only to provide its core features (managing tabs/windows, saving workspace metadata, and related UI functionality). The extension does not transmit this data to any external service.
